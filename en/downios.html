InWeCrypto

How to install InWeCrypto app (iOS)?

Scan the QR code to download

1.Download App

Open the url with Safari and install the app on your mobile phone.
(Note: if your WeChat can’t open the url, please open it with
Safari and follow the steps to install the app.)

2. Trusted Root Certificate

When the app is downloaded, user will encounter
a warning of “Untrusted App Developer”.
Then please open Settings -> General -> Device Management -> Trust App.

3. Open the App

After the app is trusted, you can open the app and start your experience.
